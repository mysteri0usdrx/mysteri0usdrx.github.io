Carlson Mapping & Analysis

Full Service GIS Research

  • Utilities - From power generation to transmission and distribution, all phases of the industry rely on geographic information to manage their operations.  GIS allows management to both see the 'Big Picture' and drill down into the small details.    
    • Power Generation, Transmission and Distribution
    • Gas Pipelines
    • Water, Storm water, Waste water
  • Wind Farm Developers - GIS is an invaluable tool for the development of a wind farm. (See our Brochure) From site identification all the way through to commissioning.  Our many years experience in wind farm development will help you with:
    • Site location - Find the areas with good wind speeds, high elevation, low ground cover and close proximity to a transmission line capable of handling power.
    • Design - Turbine placement, access roads, transmission routing.
    • Property Targeting - Find who owns the most valuable lands to allow you to build the most productive wind farm?
    • Project Status - Keep a constant watch on project development. Visualize the contracting status as land is signed up for the project.
    • Permitting - Show regulating agencies and the public that you have considered all the details surrounding your project.  Establishes good relationships.
    • Required Studies - FCC, FAA, Radar, Environmental and many more are required for approval.  See Microwave Study with topographic and aerial backgrounds (Formatted landscape - 11" X 17")
    • Setback development - Know where you can and cannot build throughout your project.  
  • Telecommunications - Assess the value of prospective tower locations.  Visualize the local network with Call Signs and Ownership.  Communicate effectively with the industry. 
  • Land Agencies - Identify parcels needed for lease agreements.  Monitor the contract status for all tracts of land needed on projects covering hundreds of miles. 
  • Business - Find new opportunities, optimal routes, competitive pressures and more.
  • Natural Resource & Environmental Firms - Streamline the permitting process.
  • Transportation - Monitor and plan for infrastructure maintenance and improvements. 
  • Military - Monitor and map infrastructure and status.  Communicate clearly with the entire chain of command.
  • Federal, State and Local Governments
